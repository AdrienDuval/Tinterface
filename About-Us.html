ABOUT TINTERFACE

Tinterface is an agency specialising in UX/UI Design. Based in india, Tinterface apply Design thinking to problem solving to come out with solutions that best serve business metrics.We believe design is about having the imagination to improve people’s lifes and the ability to harness the right technology to see it through.
